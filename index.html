About

Data-led, psychology-informed,
focused on results.

I'm a Dutch UX leader and behavioural psychologist with 20+ years turning human psychology into better digital products. My work sits at the intersection of AI, conversational design, and growth — where understanding why people behave the way they do translates directly into outcomes.

At Booking.com I was one of the initiators of the GenAI-powered AI Trip Planner, now used by millions across iOS. I've coached AI product teams in Indonesia (DANA, 190M users), designed dashboards for fast-growing Dutch AI startups, and helped brands like Air France-KLM, Coca-Cola, and ING use behavioural science to drive measurable results.

I co-founded Buyerminds — an award-winning digital design agency with a 100% focus on conversion — and ran it for 8 years across Amsterdam, Brussels, Bangkok, and Beijing.

My approach is always the same: start with psychology, design for behaviour, test everything, and ship what works.
